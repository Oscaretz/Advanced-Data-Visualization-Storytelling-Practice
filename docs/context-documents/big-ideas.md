## 1. Spatiotemporal Analysis

### **Objective**
Identify *where* and *when* engagement peaks occur to optimize timing and targeting for digital campaigns.

### **Findings**
- Engagement intensity exhibits strong **geographical variation**, with **North and Latin America** showing the highest audience retention and interaction rates.  
- Temporal analysis revealed a **consistent engagement spike between May and June**, indicating optimal periods for **campaign launches and product reveals**.  
- **Weekends** and **evening hours (18:00–23:00)** demonstrated above-average engagement performance.

### **Strategic Applications**
- **Timing Optimization:** Concentrate campaign activations in the May–June window to maximize exposure and ROI.  
- **Regional Focus:** Strengthen investment in Latin American audiences with tailored creative assets and localized messaging.  
- **Engagement Scheduling:** Post and promote during high-traffic windows to enhance content visibility.

---

## 2. Hierarchical Analysis

### **Objective**
Explore structural dominance and categorical relationships within trending content to prioritize marketing focus.

### **Findings**
- The **Music** and **Entertainment** categories dominate, representing **85% of total engagement** across regions.  
- **Subcategories** (e.g., live performances, vlogs, and short-form challenges) reveal hierarchical depth within audience preferences.  
- Content structure analysis indicates **cross-category synergies**, where entertainment and education converge in hybrid formats.

### **Strategic Applications**
- **Category Prioritization:** Allocate higher budgets toward **Music** and **Entertainment**, aligning with dominant audience interests.  
- **Content Diversification:** Introduce educational or informative narratives within entertainment-based campaigns.  
- **Trend Monitoring:** Maintain adaptive strategies to leverage emerging categories in real time.

---

## 3. Network Analysis

### **Objective**
Understand interconnections between creators and channels to identify influence hubs and potential collaborations.

### **Findings**
- Network centrality metrics identified a small number of **high-influence creators** acting as pivotal nodes within the engagement network.  
- Strong **co-viewing patterns** suggest audience overlap among entertainment and lifestyle creators.  
- **Peripheral creators** (smaller channels) maintain niche audiences with strong loyalty and high engagement ratios.

### **Strategic Applications**
- **Influencer Partnerships:** Collaborate with high-centrality creators to amplify reach and credibility.  
- **Micro-Influencer Integration:** Engage with smaller but dedicated creators to strengthen brand authenticity.  
- **Network Expansion:** Foster long-term partnerships to sustain audience growth and improve retention metrics.
